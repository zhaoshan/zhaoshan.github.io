Account Opening

Star One accounts can be opened online, by mail or by visiting one of our branches.
The procedures differ depending on what kind of identification document you have.
Use the following chart to confirm the account opening procedures required based on your circumstances.
Do you fall under any of the following?
  1. 1.Those employed at an office in Japan (excluding part-time workers)
  2. 2.Those who have resided in Japan for at least six months

*Even if you fall under the above, those who do not have a residence card or special permanent resident certificate are not eligible to apply.

Residence card or Special Permanent Resident Certificate

Accounts can only be opened at our main branch.

*In principle, the following people can open a TSB account for non-residents (foreign nationals):

  • Those who have a passport from the Republic of China (Taiwan)
  • Those who reside in Taiwan
  • Those who reside in Taiwan under applicable tax laws, and can provide official certifications (national ID card)
  • *Account opening procedures and transactions are generally conducted in person at TSB’s Head Office.
  • *Branches will not accept foreign currencies or exchange money into Japanese yen.
