FAQ

You can find the answers to many common questions about our products and services.

  • Non-residents
  • Residents

Account Opening

Q
When opening an account, if my current address is different from the address on my identification document, which address should I enter?
A

If your current address differs from the address on your identification documents when you apply to open an account, you will not be able to open an account.
To open an account, the address on your identification document must be the same as your current address.

Q
I would like the bank card and other mailed items related to opening an account to be sent to an address other than the address I stated on the application form.
A

When applying to open a Star One Account, mailed items such as bank cards will also serve as a means of verifying your identity, so we cannot send them to any address other than the one you entered.

Q
I applied to open an account. Can my family members receive registered mail that is addressed to me?
A

Only the person addressed on the registered mail can receive the mail.

Q
Can I conduct transactions without going to a branch?
A

You can conduct transactions without going to a branch.
Some products can be handled through telephone banking or internet banking.
To use internet banking, you will need to complete the user registration procedure (online sign-up).
Customers who opened a new account online after noon on Friday, August 16, 2019, will have completed the online sign-up procedure at the same time as opening their account.
After you apply for an account, we will send your internet banking login ID to your registered email address within 3 to 7 business days. Once you receive this email, you can start using Internet banking immediately.

  • *More than 7 business days may be required, depending on the application review status.
  • *If you apply to open an account online with two items of identification, you will not be able to make transfers or withdraw funds via internet banking until we have confirmed that you received your bank card.
Q
Regarding my Star One account, can I receive my bank card or the PIN notification postcard at a branch?
A

No. Star One account bank cards and their PIN notification postcards cannot be handed over at the head office or branches of Tokyo Star Bank. You can only receive them by postal mail.

Foreign Currency Deposits

Q
Can I withdraw foreign currency deposits in cash?
A

We do not handle foreign currency cash.
Foreign currency deposits cannot be withdrawn in cash.

Q
Are foreign currency deposits covered by the deposit insurance system?
A

Foreign currency deposits are not covered by the deposit insurance system.

Q
Given that foreign currency deposits are not covered by the deposit insurance system, what happens if the financial institution I deposited with goes bankrupt?
A

These will be treated the same as the portion of yen deposits that is not covered by the deposit insurance system (principal exceeding 10 million yen), and will be paid out based on the financial situation of the failed financial institution, so there is a possibility that some of the payments will be cut.
For more information, please visit the Deposit Insurance Corporation of Japan website

Q
How are foreign currency deposits taxed?
A

Of the taxes on foreign currency deposits, interest income is subject to a separate withholding tax of 20.315% (national tax 15.315% and local tax 5%).
In addition, 0.315% of national taxes are attributed to the introduction of the special income tax for reconstruction.
Exchange gains are treated as miscellaneous income and are subject to comprehensive taxation when filing tax returns.
However, salaried workers with an annual income of 20 million yen or less are not required to file if their annual income other than salary income and retirement income, including exchange gains, is 200,000 yen or less.
Exchange losses can be deducted from other positive miscellaneous income. Losses cannot be offset against other income categories.
For more information, please consult a certified public accountant or tax accountant.

Q
How can I withdraw my foreign currency deposit?
A

When withdrawing your foreign currency deposit, you can either transfer it to yen funds or have it remitted in the same currency. Withdrawals in foreign currency cash are not possible.

Q
Can I withdraw foreign currency ordinary deposits overseas in the foreign currency?
A

Foreign currency deposits held with our bank cannot be withdrawn overseas.

Q
How do I start a foreign currency deposit at Tokyo Star Bank?
A

To start making foreign currency deposits at Tokyo Star Bank, open a Star One account and deposit yen funds into the account by bank transfer or by depositing with your bank card.

To apply for an account, click here

Then, complete the procedure by visiting one of our branches, calling us via telephone banking, or using internet banking (Tokyo Star Direct). Foreign currency deposits such as US dollars, Australian dollars and euros held at other financial institutions can also be remitted to our bank in the foreign currency. However, a separate remittance fee will be charged.

Q
Can I deposit foreign currency deposits I have at other banks into Tokyo Star Bank's foreign currency deposits?
A

Yes. Foreign currency deposits such as US dollars, Australian dollars and euros held at other financial institutions can be remitted to Tokyo Star Bank bank in the foreign currency.

Services and Procedures

Q
How many days does it take for money to arrive when sending from overseas? How much are the fees?
A

We cannot provide a definite answer because the number of days it takes for the funds to arrive may vary depending on the procedures at the other bank. There are no fees when transferring funds to our bank account from overseas.

Q
I live overseas, can I use a Star Home Equity Loan?
A

The Star home equity loan is not available to customers residing overseas.

Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)

Getting Started

Q
I want to start using Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking), but an error is displayed and I cannot sign up online.
A

If you receive an error and cannot sign up online, the reason is that your name, branch number, account number, date of birth, or bank card PIN (bank card and telephone transaction PIN or telephone transaction PIN) differs from the information registered with us. Please check the error code displayed and contact us.

Q
I want to start using Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking), but I cannot check the “I agree” box in the online sign-up process.
A

On the Online Sign-up - Agreement to Terms screen, click each link for “Tokyo Star Direct Terms of Use" and "Handling of Personal Information (Privacy Statement)", and after confirming the contents, you will be able to check the consent box.
Clicking the link will open a separate window. After confirming the contents, return to the original screen and check the checkbox.

Q
Can I register login security questions in languages other than Japanese?
A

Yes, you can register in languages other than Japanese and use single-byte alphanumeric characters. However, Chinese characters cannot be entered.
Customers using non-resident accounts should enter English letters (e.g. ABC, abc) in the Japanese kana input field.

Q
Please tell me how to check if I can use Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking).
A

Contact us and we will confirm your registration details.

Q
Is a bank card required by necessity? About how long does it take to issue a bank card?
A

Even customers who do not wish to have a bank card issued can use Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking). If you do wish to have a bank card issued, please contact us. Usually, the 4-digit PIN notification and bank card will be delivered to you in about 2 weeks.

ID / Passwords

Q
What should I do if I forget my login ID, password, or security question, or if I am locked out when signing up online or logging in?
A

If you forget your login ID, password, or security question, or if you are locked out, we will reset your account. Please contact us.
Afterwards, please conduct the online sign up (initial registration) again.
Click here for information on how to do the initial registration.

Q
I was asked to enter my login security question even though I am using the same computer or smartphone.
A

To enhance security, you may be asked to enter your security question again after a certain period of time has passed or if the browser settings have been changed. We apologize for the inconvenience, but please enter your login security question to proceed.

Q
My login ID and password for Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking) have been stolen. What should I do?
A

If your login ID and password have been stolen, please immediately contact the following helpdesk to suspend services.

How to Use and Other Matters

Q
What should I do if I want to change the email address I am using for Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

If you wish to change your email address, please follow the procedure to reset the registration. In this case, we apologize for the inconvenience, but request that you contact us.

Q
Can I change my address or phone number with Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

You cannot change your address or phone number on Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking). Please contact us.

Q
Can I view all deposit and withdrawal detail statements since the account opening date with Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

You can view statements from the month before the date you completed your registration to start using Internet Banking.

Q
Menus other than balance statement and deposit/withdrawal details are giving me an error and I cannot use them.
A

Currently, the only services offered to customers using non-resident accounts (for individuals) are balance statements and deposit/withdrawal details. We plan to expand the services sequentially in the future.

Q
Can I check my past ordinary deposit account balance and transaction details via Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

You can check the details of your JPY ordinary deposit account from “Detailed Deposit/Withdrawal Statements" in Balance and Statements.
If you already have an account, you can view statements from the first day of the month prior to the date you completed the online sign-up procedure.

  1. 1You can select up to the same day of the following year of the statement start date. (e.g., from December 1, 2025 to December 1, 2026).
  2. 2If the number of statement entries exceeds 200, change the statement period and try again.
Q
Can I also check details of my current JPY time deposit, foreign currency deposit, etc., on Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

With the balance statement service, you can check the balances of your JPY ordinary deposit account, foreign currency time deposit, and JPY time deposit. However,
foreign currency ordinary deposit accounts and structured deposits are not covered by this service.

Q
Can I terminate my account from Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

You cannot complete the account termination procedure on Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking).
Please contact us for information on how to close your account.

Q
Are there any differences between the smartphone and PC versions of Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)?
A

There is no difference in the functions available on the smartphone version and the PC version.

Q
I have changed from a non-resident to a resident of Japan. Can I continue to use Tokyo Star Direct?
A

Yes, you can. Please contact us to inform us that you are now residing in Japan.

Q
What should I do if the Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking) page is not being translated?
A

Your browser may still be retaining previous website information (cache). Please clear your browser’s cache and try again.

[How to clear browser cache]

  • For PC users
    Windows: Press Ctrl-Shift-R at the same time.
    Mac: Press Command-Shift-R at the same time.
    If this does not resolve the issue, please delete your browsing history or clear the cache from your browser's settings menu.
  • For smartphone users
    iPhone/iPad (Safari): Go to Settings ＞ Apps ＞ Safari ＞ Clear History and Website Data
    Android (Chrome): Open Chrome ＞ Tap the three dots (top right menu) ＞ Select “Settings” ＞ Go to “Privacy and security” ＞ Tap “Clear browsing data” ＞ Check “Cached images and files”and tap “Clear data.”

In addition, translations may not work properly if your network is configured to block the domains of specific services such as Google, so please also check your network settings.
