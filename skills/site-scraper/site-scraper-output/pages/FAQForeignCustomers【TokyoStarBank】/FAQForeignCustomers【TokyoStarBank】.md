[Non-residents](#tab-tab-02) [Residents](#tab-tab-01) Account Opening [Q When opening an account, if my current address is different from the address on my identification document, which address should I enter?](javascript:void(0);) A If your current address differs from the address on your identification documents when you apply to open an account, you will not be able to open an account. To open an account, the address on your identification document must be the same as your current address. [Q I would like the bank card and other mailed items related to opening an account to be sent to an address other than the address I stated on the application form.](javascript:void(0);) A When applying to open a Star One Account, mailed items such as bank cards will also serve as a means of verifying your identity, so we cannot send them to any address other than the one you entered. [Q I applied to open an account. Can my family members receive registered mail that is addressed to me?](javascript:void(0);) A Only the person addressed on the registered mail can receive the mail. [Q Can I conduct transactions without going to a branch?](javascript:void(0);) A You can conduct transactions without going to a branch. Some products can be handled through telephone banking or internet banking. To use internet banking, you will need to complete the user registration procedure (online sign-up). Customers who opened a new account online after noon on Friday, August 16, 2019, will have completed the online sign-up procedure at the same time as opening their account. After you apply for an account, we will send your internet banking login ID to your registered email address within 3 to 7 business days. Once you receive this email, you can start using Internet banking immediately. *More than 7 business days may be required, depending on the application review status. *If you apply to open an account online with two items of identification, you will not be able to make transfers or withdraw funds via internet banking until we have confirmed that you received your bank card. [Q Regarding my Star One account, can I receive my bank card or the PIN notification postcard at a branch?](javascript:void(0);) A No. Star One account bank cards and their PIN notification postcards cannot be handed over at the head office or branches of Tokyo Star Bank. You can only receive them by postal mail. Foreign Currency Deposits [Q Can I withdraw foreign currency deposits in cash?](javascript:void(0);) A We do not handle foreign currency cash. Foreign currency deposits cannot be withdrawn in cash. [Q Are foreign currency deposits covered by the deposit insurance system?](javascript:void(0);) A Foreign currency deposits are not covered by the deposit insurance system. [Q Given that foreign currency deposits are not covered by the deposit insurance system, what happens if the financial institution I deposited with goes bankrupt?](javascript:void(0);) A These will be treated the same as the portion of yen deposits that is not covered by the deposit insurance system (principal exceeding 10 million yen), and will be paid out based on the financial situation of the failed financial institution, so there is a possibility that some of the payments will be cut. For more information, please visit the [Deposit Insurance Corporation of Japan website](https://www.dic.go.jp/) [Q How are foreign currency deposits taxed?](javascript:void(0);) A Of the taxes on foreign currency deposits, interest income is subject to a separate withholding tax of 20.315% (national tax 15.315% and local tax 5%). In addition, 0.315% of national taxes are attributed to the introduction of the special income tax for reconstruction. Exchange gains are treated as miscellaneous income and are subject to comprehensive taxation when filing tax returns. However, salaried workers with an annual income of 20 million yen or less are not required to file if their annual income other than salary income and retirement income, including exchange gains, is 200,000 yen or less. Exchange losses can be deducted from other positive miscellaneous income. Losses cannot be offset against other income categories. For more information, please consult a certified public accountant or tax accountant. [Q How can I withdraw my foreign currency deposit?](javascript:void(0);) A When withdrawing your foreign currency deposit, you can either transfer it to yen funds or have it remitted in the same currency. Withdrawals in foreign currency cash are not possible. [Q Can I withdraw foreign currency ordinary deposits overseas in the foreign currency?](javascript:void(0);) A Foreign currency deposits held with our bank cannot be withdrawn overseas. [Q How do I start a foreign currency deposit at Tokyo Star Bank?](javascript:void(0);) A To start making foreign currency deposits at Tokyo Star Bank, open a Star One account and deposit yen funds into the account by bank transfer or by depositing with your bank card. [To apply for an account, click here](/account/flow/) Then, complete the procedure by visiting one of our branches, calling us via telephone banking, or using internet banking (Tokyo Star Direct). Foreign currency deposits such as US dollars, Australian dollars and euros held at other financial institutions can also be remitted to our bank in the foreign currency. However, a separate remittance fee will be charged. [Q Can I deposit foreign currency deposits I have at other banks into Tokyo Star Bank's foreign currency deposits?](javascript:void(0);) A Yes. Foreign currency deposits such as US dollars, Australian dollars and euros held at other financial institutions can be remitted to Tokyo Star Bank bank in the foreign currency. Services and Procedures [Q How many days does it take for money to arrive when sending from overseas? How much are the fees?](javascript:void(0);) A We cannot provide a definite answer because the number of days it takes for the funds to arrive may vary depending on the procedures at the other bank. There are no fees when transferring funds to our bank account from overseas. [Q I live overseas, can I use a Star Home Equity Loan?](javascript:void(0);) A The Star home equity loan is not available to customers residing overseas.
