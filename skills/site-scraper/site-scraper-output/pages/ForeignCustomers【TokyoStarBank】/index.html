Guide to opening an account

Resident's account (Star One Account)

No seal or branch visit required.

Who can open an account
  • Foreign nationals who have lived in Japan for at least six months
  • Foreign nationals who want the benefits of having a bank account in Japan
Open a Star One account
  • * In branch services are provided in Japanese only; we cannot provide services in foreign languages such as English or Chinese.
  • * We reserve the right to refuse applications to open an account if your bank card, etc., cannot be delivered to the address you provided when applying to open an account, or if you are unable to accept delivery, or for other reasons where our overall judgment is that opening of an account should be refused.

Non-resident accounts

Non-resident accounts are accounts for foreign nationals that issue bank cards and permit account transfers.

  • *Account openings and all related transactions must be conducted face-to-face at our Head Office (Minato-ku, Tokyo).
  • *We do not handle foreign-currency or Japanese-yen cash exchanges at bank counters.
Who can open an account
  • Foreign nationals who are not residents of Japan
  • Those referred by a partner institution, or who plan to take out a loan at TSB
  • Those able to submit valid verification documents (official government-issued certificates)
Guide to opening a non-resident account

Request: When considering opening a non-resident account (for individuals)

  • *Account openings and all related transactions must be conducted face-to-face at our Head Office (Minato-ku, Tokyo).
  • *Please be aware that, following the screening, the account opening process may take some time, and we may occasionally be unable to fulfill your requests.

Please make a reservation in advance for your visit.
Please refrain from coming without a reservation.

We do not handle foreign-currency or Japanese-yen cash exchanges at bank counters.

Yen deposits (for non-residents)

We offer yen ordinary deposit accounts to enable you to deposit and withdraw funds, saving you from carrying around large amounts of cash, as well as yen time deposit accounts that enable you to select a fixed term over which to deposit your funds.

Who can open an account
  • Non-residents who want to use a yen ordinary deposit account to make deposits and withdrawals for living expenses
  • Non-residents who want to invest yen funds that they have no immediate plans to use
Find out more about yen deposits (for non-residents)

Foreign currency deposits (for non-residents)

We offer foreign currency deposit accounts to meet your needs.

Who can open an account
  • Foreign nationals who want a foreign currency savings account that offers fund transfer flexibility
  • Foreign nationals who want to invest money they have no immediate plans to use
Find out more about foreign currency deposits (for non-residents)
