Transaction Terms and Conditions [Tokyo Star Direct Transaction Terms and Conditions[PDF:165KB]](/tokyostardirect/regulations/pdf/tsd_disclaimer_en.pdf) FAQ Q How can I check if I can use Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking)? A Please contact a TSB representative to confirm your registered information. Q Is it absolutely necessary to have a bank card? How long does it take for a bank card to be issued? A Even customers who do not wish to have a bank card issued can use Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking). If you would like to have a bank card issued, please contact a TSB representative. It usually takes about 2 weeks for the 4-digit PIN number notification form and bank card to arrive. Q I want to start using Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking), but an error is displayed and I cannot sign up online. A If you receive an error and cannot sign up online, the reason is that your name, branch number, account number, date of birth, or bank card PIN (bank card and telephone transaction PIN or telephone transaction PIN) differs from the information registered with us. Please check the error code displayed and contact us. Q I want to start using Tokyo Star Direct (Internet Banking), but I cannot check the “I agree” box in the online sign-up process. A On the Online Sign-up - Agreement to Terms screen, click each link for “Tokyo Star Direct Terms of Use" and "Handling of Personal Information (Privacy Statement)", and after confirming the contents, you will be able to check the consent box. Clicking the link will open a separate window. After confirming the contents, return to the original screen and check the checkbox. Q What should I do if I forget my login ID, password, or security question, or if I am locked out when signing up online or logging in? A If you forget your login ID, password, or security question, or if you are locked out, we will reset your account. Please contact us. Afterwards, please conduct the online sign up (initial registration) again. Click here for information on how to do the initial registration. [New registration (Online Sign-up)](/tokyostardirect/taiken/05_olsu_en.html) Q I was asked to enter my login security question even though I am using the same computer or smartphone. A To enhance security, you may be asked to enter your security question again after a certain period of time has passed or if the browser settings have been changed. We apologize for the inconvenience, but please enter your login security question to proceed. Q Can I register login security questions in languages other than Japanese? A Yes, you can register in languages other than Japanese and use single-byte alphanumeric characters. However, Chinese characters cannot be entered. Customers using non-resident accounts should enter English letters (e.g. ABC, abc) in the Japanese kana input field.
