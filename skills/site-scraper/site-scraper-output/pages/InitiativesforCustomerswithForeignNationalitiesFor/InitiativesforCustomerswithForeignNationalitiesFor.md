Introduction to Tokyo Star Bank's Initiatives for Customers with Foreign Nationalities Supporting the growing trend of investment from overseas into Japan Although Japan is becoming increasingly attractive to overseas investors, a sufficient environment for providing smooth financial services is not yet in place. We utilize CTBC Bank's global network and collaborations with overseas financial institutions to provide unique products and services such as account opening support and real estate investment loans tailored to the diverse needs of our overseas customers. Setting a new standard for foreign nationals living in Japan The number of foreign nationals living in Japan is increasing every year, and now exceeds 3.5 million people nationwide. They come for various reasons, such as employment, study abroad, and accompanying family members, but commonly face difficulties accessing basic financial services such as opening bank accounts and applying for loans due to language and systemic barriers. We support the lives of foreign residents in Japan through financial assistance.
