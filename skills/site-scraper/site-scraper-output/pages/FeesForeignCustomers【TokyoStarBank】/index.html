Definition of a non-resident

Non-residents under the Foreign Exchange and Foreign Trade Act are foreigners who meet the following conditions.

  • 1.In principle, foreign nationals (excluding persons who work in an office in Japan and who have been in Japan for more than 6 months)
  • 2.Persons working for a foreign government or international organization
  • 3.Diplomats or consulates appointed or employed abroad and their attendants and employees
  • 4.Armed forces of United States of America or the United Nations, their personnel, civilian employees, and families, military sales organization employees, etc., and army post office employees, etc.
